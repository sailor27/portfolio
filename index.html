sailor winkelman

Sailor Winkelman is a student web designer living in Portland, OR. They have a background in technical support, project management, and digital art.

They work at People's Food Co-op as the Technology Coordinator. They attend Epicodus, where they are learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Sailor aspires to create dynamic websites that inspire curiousity and wonder from their users. They are interested in collaborating on technology projects that further grassroots social justice work.
